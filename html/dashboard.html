An Art Classification System

"Where Creativity Meets Intelligence"

About

Clevart, where creativity meets intelligence. Provides the essential needs of the user using our system that will enable us to classify the specific medium art and also it will interpret the image having an explanation to understand behind the meaning of the artwork. Additionally we add some features that will classify the specific genre of the art (e.g., abstract, impressionism, renaissance, portrait and landscape).

Mission

To give access to art appreciation and understanding by providing a user-friendly platform that utilizes cutting-edge technology to accurately identify art genre, medium, and interpretation, fostering a deeper connection between individuals and the world of art.

Vision

3D developers envision a world where everyone, regardless of their background or expertise, can confidently engage with art in all its forms. Through our innovative scanning technology, we aim to empower individuals to explore, learn, and appreciate art in a meaningful way, enriching their cultural experiences and broadening their perspectives. We aspire to be the premier destination for art enthusiasts, educators, and curious minds alike, fostering a community that celebrates the diversity and beauty of artistic expression.
